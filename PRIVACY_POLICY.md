# Privacy Policy for Voice Bot

**Last Updated: February 7, 2025**

## Introduction

Voice Bot ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our AI voice chat application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Voice Data
- **What**: Audio recordings of your voice during conversations
- **Purpose**: To convert speech to text and enable voice chat functionality
- **Storage**: Processed locally on your device and sent to third-party services for transcription
- **Retention**: Not stored permanently; processed in real-time only

### 2. Chat Messages
- **What**: Text of your conversations with the AI
- **Purpose**: To maintain conversation context and provide relevant responses
- **Storage**: Stored locally on your device only
- **Retention**: Kept until you clear the conversation or delete the app

### 3. User Preferences
- **What**: Your selected language, voice provider, and other settings
- **Purpose**: To personalize your experience
- **Storage**: Stored locally on your device
- **Retention**: Kept until you change settings or delete the app

### 4. Device Information
- **What**: Device type, iOS version, app version
- **Purpose**: For troubleshooting and improving app performance
- **Storage**: Only collected during crash reports (if enabled)

## Third-Party Services

Voice Bot integrates with the following third-party services to provide its functionality:

### 1. OpenAI API
- **Purpose**: AI conversation responses
- **Data Shared**: Your chat messages
- **Privacy Policy**: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
- **Note**: Conversations are processed according to OpenAI's data usage policies

### 2. Speech Recognition (Apple)
- **Purpose**: Convert your voice to text
- **Data Shared**: Audio recordings
- **Privacy Policy**: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
- **Note**: Processed using on-device and cloud-based Apple services

### 3. ElevenLabs (Optional TTS Provider)
- **Purpose**: Text-to-speech voice generation
- **Data Shared**: Text responses from AI
- **Privacy Policy**: https://elevenlabs.io/privacy
- **Note**: Only used if you select ElevenLabs as your voice provider

### 4. BOTNOI Voice (Optional TTS Provider)
- **Purpose**: Text-to-speech for Thai language
- **Data Shared**: Text responses from AI
- **Privacy Policy**: https://botnoi.ai/privacy
- **Note**: Only used if you select BOTNOI Voice as your provider

## How We Use Your Information

1. **Provide Core Functionality**: Enable voice chat conversations with AI
2. **Improve User Experience**: Remember your preferences and settings
3. **Troubleshooting**: Diagnose and fix technical issues
4. **Service Improvement**: Understand usage patterns to enhance features

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage**: Most data is stored locally on your device using secure iOS storage
- **Encryption**: Data transmitted to third-party APIs is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- **No Cloud Storage**: We do not store your conversations or voice data on our servers
- **API Keys**: Your API keys are stored securely on your device

## Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

1. **Access**: View all data stored locally in the app
2. **Delete**: Clear conversation history at any time within the app
3. **Control**: Choose which services to use (OpenAI, ElevenLabs, BOTNOI Voice)
4. **Opt-Out**: Stop using the app and delete it to remove all local data

## Children's Privacy

Voice Bot is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Data Retention

- **Conversation History**: Stored locally until you clear it or delete the app
- **Voice Recordings**: Not retained; processed in real-time only
- **Settings**: Stored locally until you change them or delete the app
- **Cached Audio**: TTS audio cache is stored locally for faster playback; cleared when you delete the app

## International Data Transfers

By using Voice Bot, you acknowledge that your data may be transferred to and processed by third-party services located in different countries, including:
- OpenAI (United States)
- ElevenLabs (United States)
- BOTNOI Voice (Thailand)

These services comply with applicable data protection regulations.
