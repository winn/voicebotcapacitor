Privacy Policy for Voice Bot

Last Updated: February 7, 2025

Introduction

Voice Bot ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our AI voice chat application.

Information We Collect

1. Voice Data

  • What: Audio recordings of your voice during conversations
  • Purpose: To convert speech to text and enable voice chat functionality
  • Storage: Processed locally on your device and sent to third-party services for transcription
  • Retention: Not stored permanently; processed in real-time only

2. Chat Messages

  • What: Text of your conversations with the AI
  • Purpose: To maintain conversation context and provide relevant responses
  • Storage: Stored locally on your device only
  • Retention: Kept until you clear the conversation or delete the app

3. User Preferences

  • What: Your selected language, voice provider, and other settings
  • Purpose: To personalize your experience
  • Storage: Stored locally on your device
  • Retention: Kept until you change settings or delete the app

Third-Party Services

Voice Bot integrates with the following third-party services:

1. OpenAI API

  • Purpose: AI conversation responses
  • Data Shared: Your chat messages
  • Privacy Policy: OpenAI Privacy Policy

2. Speech Recognition (Apple)

  • Purpose: Convert your voice to text
  • Data Shared: Audio recordings
  • Privacy Policy: Apple Privacy Policy

3. ElevenLabs (Optional)

  • Purpose: Text-to-speech voice generation
  • Privacy Policy: ElevenLabs Privacy

4. BOTNOI Voice (Optional)

  • Purpose: Text-to-speech for Thai language
  • Privacy Policy: BOTNOI Privacy

How We Use Your Information

  1. Provide Core Functionality: Enable voice chat conversations with AI
  2. Improve User Experience: Remember your preferences and settings
  3. Troubleshooting: Diagnose and fix technical issues
  4. Service Improvement: Understand usage patterns to enhance features

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Most data is stored locally on your device
  • Encryption: Data transmitted to APIs is encrypted (HTTPS/TLS)
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your conversations on our servers
  • API Keys: Stored securely on your device only

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored locally in the app
  • Delete: Clear conversation history at any time
  • Control: Choose which services to use
  • Opt-Out: Delete the app to remove all local data

Children's Privacy

Voice Bot is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your API Keys

Important:

  • Your API keys are stored securely on your device only
  • We never transmit, collect, or store your API keys
  • You are responsible for keeping your API keys secure
